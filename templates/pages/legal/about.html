About tapne

tapne is a travel creator platform where hosts share trips, publish stories, and grow communities.

What you can do here

The same core actions that shape the Lovable prototype are available here in Django.

Explore trips

Browse hosted trips, compare details, and request to join when you find the right fit.

Follow people

Track creators, hosts, and other members across profiles, bookmarks, and direct messages.

Read stories

Use community blogs, comments, and reviews to understand the people behind the trips.

Core product loop

  • Guests discover trips and content.
  • Members unlock messaging, reviews, bookmarks, and join requests.
  • Hosts publish trips and manage applications through the same visual system.
