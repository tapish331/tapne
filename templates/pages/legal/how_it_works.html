How tapne works

Guests can browse trips and blogs. Members unlock join requests, messaging, comments, reviews, and bookmarks.

For guests

  • Browse trips, creators, and blogs.
  • Open detail pages to understand trip fit and host style.
  • Create an account when you are ready to participate.

For members and hosts

  • Members can request to join trips and interact across the platform.
  • Hosts can publish trips, review requests, and share updates.
  • Creators can publish blogs and build a public audience.
