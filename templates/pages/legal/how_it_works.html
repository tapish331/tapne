How tapne works

Guests can browse trips and blogs. Members unlock join requests, messaging, comments, reviews, and bookmarks.

For guests

Discovery first, account creation only when participation becomes necessary.

Browse

Review trips, hosts, destinations, and blogs before making any commitment.

Compare

Open details pages to understand trip fit, logistics, vibe, and host style.

Join later

Create an account only when you want to interact, review, or request a seat.

For members and hosts

Once signed in, the experience expands into participation and management flows.

Members

Request to join trips, bookmark creators, write reviews, and message directly.

Hosts

Create trips, review applications, and manage hosted experiences.

Creators

Publish blog stories and build a public audience around your travel style.
