Trust and safety

We want trip planning and community interactions on tapne to be respectful, transparent, and safe.

Community expectations

These are the baseline norms across trips, blogs, comments, messages, and reviews.

Be accurate

Use clear, honest trip and profile information so members know what to expect.

Be respectful

Treat other members with respect in comments, messages, and host interactions.

Do no harm

Do not post abusive, deceptive, or unsafe content or behavior.

Report concerns

If you encounter abusive behavior or suspicious activity, contact us directly.
